                <p class="lead">
                  A well-maintained boat is not only a symbol of safety but also represents long-term savings. At <strong>Rumbomed</strong>, we know that the sea does not forgive a lack of foresight.
                </p>

                <p>
                  Saltwater, humidity, and constant use demand a rigorous care routine. Whether it's a rental sailboat or your own motorboat, preventive maintenance is the key to avoiding costly breakdowns in the middle of a relaxing day.
                </p>

                <div class="highlight-quote">
                  <blockquote role="quote">
                    <p>At sea, the best improvisation is the one born from an impeccable inspection at the port.</p>
                    <cite>Rumbomed Captaincy</cite>

              <div class="content-section" id="engine" data-aos="fade-up">
                <h2>The Heart of the Vessel: The Engine</h2>
                <div class="image-with-caption right">
                  <img src="/assets/img/blog/mantenimiento-motores.webp" alt="Marine mechanic performing filter and fluid level checks" class="img-fluid" loading="lazy">
                  <figcaption>Checking filters and levels is vital before every departure.</figcaption>
                </div>
                <p>
                  Marine engines operate under extreme conditions. Preventive maintenance includes oil changes, fuel filter inspections, and checking the raw water cooling system.
                </p>

                <div class="feature-points">
                  <div class="point">
                    <i class="bi bi-droplet-half" aria-hidden="true"></i>
                    <div>
                      <h4>Fluids and Filters</h4>
                      <p>Monitoring oil, coolant, and water separators in the fuel system.</p>
                    </div>
                  </div>
                  <div class="point">
                    <i class="bi bi-fan" aria-hidden="true"></i>
                    <div>
                      <h4>Impeller</h4>
                      <p>Annual replacement of the water pump impeller to prevent overheating.</p>
                    </div>
                  </div>
                </div>
              </div>

              <div class="content-section" id="hull" data-aos="fade-up">
                <h2>Hull Care and Sacrificial Anodes</h2>
                <p>
                  Galvanic protection is critical. Without anodes in good condition, electrolysis will corrode the metal parts of your engine and hull. We recommend a visual inspection every quarter.
                </p>
                
                <div class="comparison-grid">
                  <div class="row g-4">
                    <div class="col-md-6">
                      <div class="comparison-card">
                        <div class="icon"><i class="bi bi-shield-check"></i></div>
                        <h4>Key Points</h4>
                        <ul>
                          <li>Cleaning the hull (algae and barnacles)</li>
                          <li>Condition of the antifouling paint</li>
                          <li>Inspection of seacocks and thru-hulls</li>
                        </ul>
                      </div>
                    </div>
                    <div class="col-md-6">
                      <div class="comparison-card">
                        <div class="icon"><i class="bi bi-exclamation-triangle"></i></div>
                        <h4>Warning Signs</h4>
                        <ul>
                          <li>Anodes worn more than 50%</li>
                          <li>Signs of osmosis (blistering)</li>
                          <li>Unusual vibrations in the propeller</li>
                        </ul>
                      </div>
                    </div>
