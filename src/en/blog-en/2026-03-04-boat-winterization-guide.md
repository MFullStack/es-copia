            <div class="content-section" id="introduction" data-aos="fade-up">
              <p class="lead">
                Winterizing is not just about parking your boat; it’s about ensuring it survives the humidity and cold of the Mediterranean winter. At <strong>Rumbomed</strong>, we believe proper storage is the secret to a long-lasting vessel.
              </p>

              <p>
                In the Costa del Sol, although the climate is mild, humidity and inactivity can be the worst enemies of your boat's electrical systems and upholstery. A structured winterization plan saves you thousands in repairs.
              </p>

              <div class="highlight-quote">
                <blockquote role="quote">
                  <p>A boat that is ignored during winter will demand your full attention (and wallet) in the spring.</p>
                  <cite>Rumbomed Captaincy</cite>
                </blockquote>
              </div>
            </div>

            <div class="content-section" id="engine-storage" data-aos="fade-up">
              <h2>Engine and Fuel System</h2>
              <div class="image-with-caption right">
                <img src="/assets/img/blog/mantenimiento-motor-exterior.webp" alt="Protecting the marine engine for winter" class="img-fluid" loading="lazy">
                <figcaption>Stabilizing fuel and flushing the cooling system is vital.</figcaption>
              </div>
              <p>
                The engine needs special attention before a long period of inactivity. We recommend changing the oil to remove acids and adding a fuel stabilizer to prevent the "varnish" effect in the tank.
              </p>

              <div class="feature-points">
                <div class="point">
                  <i class="bi bi-fuel-pump" aria-hidden="true"></i>
                  <div>
                    <h4>Fuel Treatment</h4>
                    <p>Keep the tank 95% full with stabilizer to minimize condensation.</p>
                  </div>
                </div>
                <div class="point">
                  <i class="bi bi-battery-charging" aria-hidden="true"></i>
                  <div>
                    <h4>Battery Maintenance</h4>
                    <p>Disconnect and charge them periodically or use a solar trickle charger.</p>
                  </div>
                </div>
              </div>
            </div>

            <div class="content-section" id="interior-care" data-aos="fade-up">
              <h2>Interior Care: Fighting Humidity</h2>
              <p>
                Mildew can ruin an interior in weeks. Remove all cushions, life jackets, and fabrics, or at least place them in a vertical position to allow air circulation.
              </p>
              
              <div class="comparison-grid">
                <div class="row g-4">
                  <div class="col-md-6">
                    <div class="comparison-card">
                      <div class="icon"><i class="bi bi-wind"></i></div>
                      <h4>Ventilation</h4>
                      <ul>
                        <li>Use moisture absorbers (calcium chloride)</li>
                        <li>Keep interior doors and lockers open</li>
                        <li>Clean the bilge and keep it dry</li>
                      </ul>
                    </div>
                  </div>
                  <div class="col-md-6">
                    <div class="comparison-card">
                      <div class="icon"><i class="bi bi-droplet-slash"></i></div>
                      <h4>Plumbing</h4>
                      <ul>
                        <li>Drain fresh water tanks</li>
                        <li>Empty the water heater</li>
                        <li>Clean and dry the holding tanks</li>
                      </ul>
                    </div>
                  </div>
                </div>
