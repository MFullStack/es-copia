            <div class="content-section" id="introduction" data-aos="fade-up">
              <p class="lead">
                The Costa del Sol is more than just luxury and beaches; it is a vital corridor for marine life. At <strong>Rumbomed</strong>, we take you to the heart of the Alboran Sea to witness dolphins and whales in their natural habitat.
              </p>

              <p>
                From the playful Common Dolphin to the majestic Fin Whale, our routes are designed to maximize spotting opportunities while respecting the marine environment.
              </p>

              <div class="highlight-quote">
                <blockquote role="quote">
                  <p>"There is nothing quite like the sound of a dolphin's blowhole breaking the silence of the Mediterranean morning."</p>
                  <cite>Rumbomed Expedition Leader</cite>
                </blockquote>
              </div>
            </div>

            <div class="content-section" id="benalmadena" data-aos="fade-up">
              <h2>Benalmádena: The Family Favorite</h2>
              <p>
                Departing from the award-winning <strong>Puerto Marina</strong>, this route is known for its high concentration of Common and Striped dolphins. The coastal shelf here provides the perfect feeding ground.
              </p>

              <div class="feature-points">
                <div class="point">
                  <i class="bi bi-geo-alt" aria-hidden="true"></i>
                  <div>
                    <h4>Strategic Spotting</h4>
                    <p>High success rate within just 2 miles of the coast.</p>
                  </div>
                </div>
                <div class="point">
                  <i class="bi bi-people" aria-hidden="true"></i>
                  <div>
                    <h4>Family Friendly</h4>
                    <p>Calmer waters ideal for children and first-time sailors.</p>
                  </div>
                </div>
              </div>
            </div>

            <div class="content-section" id="marbella" data-aos="fade-up">
              <h2>Marbella & Banús: Deep Sea Wonders</h2>
              <div class="image-with-caption right">
                <img src="/assets/img/blog/marbella-sailing.webp" alt="Sailing boat in Marbella looking for cetaceans" class="img-fluid" loading="lazy">
                <figcaption>Deep waters near Marbella often hide Pilot Whales.</figcaption>
              </div>
              <p>
                Sailing from Marbella allows us to reach greater depths quickly. Here, we often encounter the <strong>Bottlenose Dolphin</strong>, known for its size and curiosity towards boats.
              </p>
              <p>
                During the summer months, keep your eyes on the horizon; the deep trenches in this area are part of the migratory path for larger cetaceans.
              </p>
            </div>

            <div class="content-section" id="estepona" data-aos="fade-up">
              <h2>Estepona: The Gateway to the Strait</h2>
              <p>
                Estepona offers our most adventurous route. Its proximity to the Strait of Gibraltar means nutrient-rich currents that attract the "giants" of the sea.
              </p>
